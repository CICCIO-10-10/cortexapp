# Cortex — AI Study App for Italian Students

> **L'app AI che ti prepara alla maturità italiana** · [cortexapp.it](https://cortexapp.it)

Cortex is a web app that helps Italian high school students prepare for the *maturità* (final exam) using AI-powered study tools.

---

## What is Cortex?

Cortex combines spaced repetition, AI interrogation, and gamification into a single study platform built specifically for the Italian school system.

**Live at → [cortexapp.it](https://cortexapp.it)**

---

## Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| 🧠 **Neural Coach** | AI tutor powered by Gemini Flash. Upload your notes, ask questions, get answers based on *your* material |
| 🃏 **Flashcard SRS** | Spaced repetition system (SM-2 algorithm) — shows you what you need to review at the right time |
| ⚔️ **BossMode** | Simulates the oral exam commission. Random questions, timer, immediate feedback |
| 🗺️ **MindMap** | Visualize connections between concepts in your study material |
| 🏆 **Duel Mode** | Real-time quiz battles against other students |
| 🔥 **Streak & XP** | Daily streaks and experience points to keep you consistent |

## Why Cortex?

The Italian *maturità* is a high-stakes exam covering 5 years of school material across multiple subjects. Students need:
1. A system to organize their notes
2. A way to test themselves efficiently
3. Something that adapts to what they don't know yet

Cortex solves all three — and makes studying less boring.
